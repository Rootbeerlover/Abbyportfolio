About & Contact

Abby Rodriguez is a multimedia artist, who is curious and always wanting to learn more.
Her artwork addresses seeing oneself by erasing the individual and connecting to the whole.
She frequently explores the act of observation and timing, including different forms of resistance,
the documentation of social movements and the navigation of being Chicana in the U.S. today.

She has received her Bachelors in Art & Design from Cal State East Bay.
As well as an associates in Graphic Design, Studio Arts and Liberal Arts from Chabot College.

While at Cal State East Bay she wrote 3 articles for the school newspaper, The Pioneer. Including articles such as Hardcore Punk Graces Hayward, Kirk Leclaire Profile, and Orbit Skate & Boutique . With her Kirk Leclaire profile featured in the Hayward Herald.

She had her work featured in the Annual Juried Student Exhibition at Cal State East Bay in 2025 and 2026. Winning the Fred English Photography Award in 2025.

Her photograph was also selected for a photobook by Photoworks Guest Edition 2 in 2025.
